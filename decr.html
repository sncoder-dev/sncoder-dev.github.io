ABOUT THE GAME

This game is a great way to enhance your mathematical and logical skills while enjoying the fun of gaming. In this game, you will be presented with a square grid containing small squares. Each small square contains a number from 1 to 9. Your goal is to click on the squares to create a sequence of numbers that adds up to the given question. The game is not only an entertaining game but also an excellent way to sharpen your logical thinking and computational abilities.
